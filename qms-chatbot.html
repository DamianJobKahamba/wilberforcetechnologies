An AI-powered Quality Management System assistant designed specifically for healthcare laboratories in Tanzania. Built with ISO 15189 compliance and multilingual support to enhance laboratory quality assurance practices.

Try Live Demo View on GitHub

Revolutionizing Laboratory Quality Management

The QMS Chatbot bridges the gap between complex quality management standards and practical daily operations in healthcare laboratories across Tanzania and beyond.

ISO 15189 Compliance

Built-in guidance for medical laboratory quality standards, ensuring your laboratory meets international accreditation requirements with real-time assistance.

Multilingual Support

Native support for English and Kiswahili, making quality management accessible to all laboratory staff regardless of their preferred language.

Real-time Guidance

Instant answers to quality management questions, from sample collection protocols to equipment maintenance schedules and staff training requirements.

Training Integration

Comprehensive training modules and assessment tools to help laboratory staff understand and implement quality management best practices.

Document Management

Intelligent document retrieval system that helps staff find relevant SOPs, protocols, and quality manuals quickly and efficiently.

Audit Preparation

Automated checklist generation and compliance tracking to help laboratories prepare for internal and external quality audits.

Technical Specifications

Platform: Django/Python Web Application
AI Engine: Hybrid Ollama/Groq Routing
Database: PostgreSQL (migrating from SQLite)
Languages: English, Kiswahili
Standards: ISO 15189 Compliant
Deployment: Cloud-hosted at qms.damianchatbot.net
Security: Enterprise-grade authentication
License: Open Source

Primary Use Cases

Laboratory Staff Training

New staff members can quickly get up to speed with quality management protocols through interactive Q&A sessions and guided tutorials.

Daily Operations Support

Staff can get instant answers about sample handling, equipment maintenance, and protocol adherence during their daily work.

Audit Preparation

Laboratory managers can use the system to ensure compliance readiness and generate comprehensive audit preparation checklists.

Knowledge Management

Centralized access to all quality documents, SOPs, and training materials with intelligent search and retrieval capabilities.
